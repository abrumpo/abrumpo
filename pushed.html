Mobile Push Notification Software



This exciting new mobile push notification software solution provides your company with the ability to send push notifications to your mobile applications.


Platforms Supported:
iOS (Apple Push Notification System - APNS)
Android (Google Cloud Messaging - GCM)


On Premises



Install locally, in your data center or in your cloud, but you control it

iOS & Android



Apple Push Notification System - APNS
Google Cloud Messaging - GCM

Encrypted Payloads



Pushed also supports encryption, allowing accompanying data to be encrypted server side and decrypted within your application with a secure key that was generated uniquely for the device.

Local or Active Directory Users



Utilize the built in user management system or your server's access to Active Directory to authenticate users. Anonymous works too!


Pushed increases business productivity and efficiency by sending targeted, smart push notifications that employees can quickly and easily retrieve via their mobile device, without needing to remember logon credentials or access multiple sites.

With Pushed you can establish smart-messaging that keeps your target audience engaged with up-to-date, pertinent information all from within your mobile application.




Avoid email clutter and the risk of losing important messages within overfull inboxes.

Example Use Cases


Pushed can be included in a new mobile application or added to an existing one


Notifications



Create a simple notifications mobile app to alert users to important information:

Notify team members of a deadline

Notify end users that an update of an application is ready



Time Approval



Do you have a time approval application?

Add Pushed to your application and notify approvers that they have time to approve



Company News



Delivering company news via email and it's not getting read?

Develop a mobile app that consolidates all your notifications in one place and notifies users when there is new content
